---

## Obliczenia
---

$$
\begin{align}
P&=\frac{U^2}{R} \\ \\
P_{1}&=10W& P_{2} =? \\
10&=\frac{U^2}{R_{1}}& P_{2}=\frac{U^2}{2R_{1}} \\
10 \cdot R_{1}&=U^2&\implies P_{2}=\frac{10 \cdot R_{1}}{2R_{1}} \\
P_{2}&=\frac{10}{2}=5W \\ \\
\end{align}
$$
$$
P_{2}=5W
